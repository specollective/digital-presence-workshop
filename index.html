What is your digital presence?

Your digital presence is the space that your brand owns online. (1)

  • Your website
  • Your company social media accounts
  • Your listings in business directories
  • The searches that your website shows up for
  • Any organizations, events, and publications that you actively participate in (1)

What is a personal brand?

Your personal brand identifies "who you are as a leader, how you treat people, how you want to be known – in and out of the office" (2)

What are remote collaboration tools?

  • Asynchronous chat tools like Slack and MS Teams
  • Google Suite and Office 360 (docs, spreadsheets, etc)
  • Project management software like Trello and Jira
  • Side builders / CMS like WP, Squarespace, & Wix
  • Code hosting platforms like Github and Bitucket

How are remote collaboration tools related to your digital brand?

Work is increasingly happening online

  • Using remote collaboration tools is part of your digital presence
  • Communicating online affects how people perceive your digital brand
  • You will be demonstrating your leadership and professionalism online

How can you prepare for remote work experiences?

  • Try free / trial versions of these tools
  • Use Slack for your friend group chats
  • Manage your personal projects using Trello
  • Create a professional signature for your email account
  • Build a personal website using a site builder
  • Use a search engine to find examples of other people's digital brands
  • Find small contracts in your respective fields
